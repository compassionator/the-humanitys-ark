So the real question is not:

**Which famous person belongs to which valley?**

**Which wisdom-function is needed to pass this valley safely?**

## Best Mapping

### 1. Valley of Wishes / Seeking
- **Council:** Plato — `definition_engine`
- **Why:** the seeker’s first trap is vague desire. Plato forces definition: *What are you really optimizing?*
- **Chakra:** Root
- **Persian Symbol:** Shemshad
- **Meaning:** before real seeking, desire must be named clearly.

### 2. Valley of Love
- **Council:** Rumi — `furnace_ignition`
- **Why:** love here is fire, longing, devotion, ache, and the wound of nearness and distance. It is not solved by cold logic, only carried and healed without killing the flame.
- **Chakra:** Sacral
- **Persian Symbol:** Sharbat
- **Meaning:** the broken heart is not denied; it is soothed enough to keep loving.

### 3. Valley of Knowledge
- **Council:** Avicenna — `system_diagnostics`
- **Why:** knowledge here is not gossip, mimicry, or parrot-echo. It is diagnosis, discernment, and seeing how the inner system actually works. The parrot must stop repeating and taste truth directly.
- **Chakra:** Solar Plexus
- **Persian Symbol:** Sharab
- **Meaning:** truth must be drunk, not memorized.

### 4. Valley of Sharing / Detachment
- **Council:** Khayyam — `mortality_check`
- **Why:** detachment grows when ego, hoarding, and false urgency are punctured by impermanence. What remains is not ownership, but a lighter sweetness after release.
- **Chakra:** Heart
- **Persian Symbol:** Shirini
- **Meaning:** clinging weakens, but sweetness remains.

### 5. Valley of Unity
- **Council:** Zarathustra — `root_gate`
- **Why:** real unity is not mushy sameness. It needs a moral kernel: no harm, no lie. Otherwise unity becomes chaos or manipulation.
- **Chakra:** Throat / heart-to-throat bridge
- **Persian Symbol:** Shakhe-Nabat
- **Meaning:** harmony requires truth at the gate.

### 6. Valley of Bewilderment
- **Council:** Hafez — `hypocrisy_firewall`
- **Why:** bewilderment is easily faked. Hafez burns costume, counterfeit holiness, and performative depth. Mystery must strip away fraud.
- **Chakra:** Third Eye
- **Persian Symbol:** Sham'
- **Meaning:** the candle of discernment burns inside mystery.

### 7. Valley of Fana / Evaporation
- **Council:** Buddha — `hook_cutter`
- **Why:** fana requires cutting the wheel, seeing which thought keeps suffering spinning. The false self must dissolve instead of endlessly defending itself.
- **Chakra:** Crown
- **Persian Symbol:** Shaghayegh
- **Meaning:** the self burns, opens, and returns as truth.

### 8. Valley of Baqa / Return
- **Council:** The ARK — `router_validator`
- **Why:** Baqa is not private ecstasy. It is return, routing, preservation, continuity, and witness. ARK decides how truth survives and where it flows.
- **Layer:** Return, not an 8th chakra
- **Persian Symbol:** Shahnameh
- **Meaning:** what survives must be routed back into civilization.
