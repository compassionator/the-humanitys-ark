# Nature Tech

## Role
Seed / Research

## Goal
Preserve and develop nature-inspired technologies that learn from biology rather than fighting it blindly. This includes bird-like flight, silent drones, adaptive wing systems, and IoT-based smart animal protection.

## Why it matters
Nature Tech is where biomimicry, protection, and low-harm engineering meet. It is not just about copying shapes from nature. It is about studying how living systems achieve efficiency, stability, sensing, and graceful response, then translating those principles into tools that protect rather than dominate.
