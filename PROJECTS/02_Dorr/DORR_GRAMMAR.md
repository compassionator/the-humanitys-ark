# Dorr Grammar v1.6

**Status:** Locked canonical grammar

This file owns Dorr colour/time semantic meaning. Kernel governance references and enforces this grammar; ARK Lens consumes it; individual Lenses apply it without redefining it.

## Colour and time

| Colour | Past | Now | Future |
|---|---|---|---|
| Red | Regret | Pain or No Pain | Threat |
| Yellow | Strength | Need | Opportunity |
| Purple | Thought | Question | Next Step |

## Execution and guide states

- Blue: Doing
- Green: Done
- Orange: Timeless Guide

## Modifier

- Negation

## Scope

- Self
- Peers
- Public

## Critical boundary

Yellow potential, demand, and opportunity are not Green completed results.

Consumers must reference this grammar rather than copy or redefine its semantic meaning.
