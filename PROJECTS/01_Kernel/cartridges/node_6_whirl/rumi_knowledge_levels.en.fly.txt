Those who know and yearn to know they know,
Shall ascend to the heights where joy does grow.

Those who know and know that they know,
Their steed of wisdom, through the sky's dome, will joyously go.

Those who know and yet don't grasp that they know,
With a jug of water, in thirst's grip, they remain low.

Those who don't know and yearn to know,
Shall free soul & body from ignorance's shadow.

Those who don't know and know that they don't know,
Their limping beast shall reach the goal, in wisdom's glow.

Those who don't know and don't know that they don't know,
In eternal ignorance's compound, they'll forever undergo.

Those who don't know and firmly decline to know,
It's a pity such creatures in life's dance barely show.
