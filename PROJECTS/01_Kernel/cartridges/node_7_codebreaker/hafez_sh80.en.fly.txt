Blame not the free-spirited, O saint so pure,
For their faults on your record won't endure.
Be I a sinner or not, mind your own course,
Each reaps what they sow, for better or worse.
All souls seek love, both wise and wild,
In every place, love's home is established—
From mosque to fire temple.
Do not despair me of eternal grace;
Behind the veil, who truly knows
Who is fair—and who only shows?
