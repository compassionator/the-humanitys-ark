Truly there are two primal Spirits, twins renowned to be in conflict. In thought and word and act they are two: the Good and the Bad. And of these two, the wise do choose aright, but the foolish do not.
When these two Spirits came together at the beginning, they established Life and Not-Life, and that at the last the Worst Existence shall be to the followers of the Lie (Druj), but the Best Mind to him that follows Right (Asha).

He who upholds the Truth (Asha) with all the might of his life, he helps the creation of Good Mind (Vohu Manah). But he who is false and deceives, he destroys the very foundation of the living world.
O Mazda, when Thou first didst create our life and our conscience and our understanding, Thou didst establish the freedom of the will: that the free agent might act according to his own choice.
Therefore, listen to the Soul of the Earth (Geush Urva), who complains: "Cruelty and violence and the Lie have surrounded me. I have no protector but Thee."

Maxim: Humata, Hukhta, Hvarshta. (Good Thoughts, Good Words, Good Deeds.)
There is only one path, and that is the path of Truth. All other paths are no paths.
