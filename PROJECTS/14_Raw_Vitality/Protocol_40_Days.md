A recurring 40-day period of raw plant-based living may act as a reset or cleansing cycle and may improve vitality for some people.

## Historical protocol description
The preserved hypothesis describes a 40-day period with:
- no animal products
- no cooked foods
- no fried foods
- no processed junk
- no routine chemical food inputs where avoidable
- favor raw fruits, vegetables, sprouts, nuts, seeds, and simple natural intake
- support with better breathing and more natural movement
