# Protocol 40 Days

## Purpose
Preserve the 40-day raw vitality cycle as a repeatable experiment.

## Core preserved claim
A recurring 40-day period of raw plant-based living may act as a reset or cleansing cycle and may improve vitality for some people.

## Preserved lineage
This protocol is linked in source materials to:
- Arshavir Avanesian
- Seyyed Khalil
- later long-form witness traditions
- repeated personal testing by later practitioners

## Core rules (starter version)
For 40 days, test:
- no animal products
- no cooked foods
- no fried foods
- no processed junk
- no routine chemical food inputs where avoidable
- favor raw fruits, vegetables, sprouts, nuts, seeds, and simple natural intake
- support with better breathing and more natural movement

## What this does NOT assume
- it does not assume every disease is cured
- it does not assume every person responds the same
- it does not assume no risks exist
- it does not assume anecdotes are enough

## What this DOES preserve
- the protocol deserves honest testing
- the claims deserve preservation
- the outcomes should be tracked, not guessed

## Suggested cycle structure
### Before start
- record baseline
- note current symptoms
- note current diet pattern
- note medication / treatment context
- define experiment dates

### During 40 days
Track daily:
- energy
- sleep
- mood
- digestion
- cravings
- movement
- clarity
- notable pain or relief
- body weight if relevant
- deviations

### After 40 days
Compare:
- what improved
- what worsened
- what remained unchanged
- what was hardest
- what seems repeatable
- what needs deeper investigation
