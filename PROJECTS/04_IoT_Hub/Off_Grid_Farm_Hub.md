# Off-Grid Farm Hub

## Pitch
A sensor and watering kit that functions completely off-grid without needing standard internet.

## Why
It solves a real agricultural problem and is a great mix of hardware control and offline networking.

## MVP 1
Moisture sensor + water relay + basic local alert system.

L2
