# Links

This file preserves the wider Harry <> Bob device stack that belongs around the IoT Hub.

## 1. Portable Tanboor Tuner
- custom physical tuner for real-world playing
- MVP: detect pitch and show tuning clearly
- Complexity: L1

## 2. Smart Noise Filter
- wearable audio bridge that filters junk and preserves crucial sounds
- MVP: mic -> phone -> classification -> earbuds
- Complexity: L1-L2

## 3. Off-Grid Smart Farm Hub
- off-grid sensor and watering kit
- MVP: moisture sensor + relay + local alerts
- Complexity: L2

## 4. Smart Bird Guardian
- danger-listening/watching protection box with physical color alerts
- MVP: detect movement/sound -> trigger Green/Yellow/Red status
- Complexity: L2

## 5. Mini Private AI Camera
- tiny camera for home, car, or wearable use with local image detection
- MVP: simple local detection such as person, pet, or motion
- Complexity: L2-L3
- Future: hat / wrist / necklace form factors, richer on-device AI

## 6. Portable Solar + Battery Field Power
- portable clean power modules for field devices
- MVP: one reliable power box for a small IoT device
- Complexity: L2
- Future: smart charging logic, modular battery packs, larger farm integration

## 7. Budget Pro ASMR Mic
- low-cost but high-fidelity audio tools for sensitive sound capture
- MVP: clean mic capsule + low-noise amplifier experiment
- Complexity: L2
- Future: stereo or binaural rigs, portable creator tools

## 8. Kid-Safe Communicator
- child-safe communication device limited to voice, video, and icons
- MVP: simple push-to-talk or icon messaging between pre-approved devices
- Complexity: L2-L3
- Future: AI guardrails, auto-summaries, parent controls

## 9. Smart Warehouse Helpers
- practical helper devices for logistics spaces
- MVP: one narrow use case such as a smart trolley for elderly or worker assist
- Sub-branches: assist trolley, robotic claw on rails, smart pigeon / parcel box
- Complexity: L2-L3

## 10. AI / AR Truck Assistant
- AI co-driver that provides useful voice and awareness help for truck drivers
- MVP: voice-only situational assistant
- Complexity: L3
- Future: AR overlays, safety prompts, site guidance

## 11. Real-World AI + AR System
- hardware/software setup integrating AI voice assistance with real-world AR
- MVP: one narrow assist use case using voice and basic AR markers
- Complexity: L3
- Future: full multi-purpose platform

## 12. Biotech / Sound & Vibration Devices
- custom devices for sensing, measuring, and experimenting with sound and vibration
- MVP: basic sensing, display, and simple measurement of local vibrations
- Complexity: L3
- Future: richer biological or environmental resonance experiments

## 13. Nature-Driven Drones
- biomimicry flight concepts based on birds or bats, not only brute-force propellers
- MVP: concept prototype or wing-motion / airflow study
- Complexity: L4
- Future: advanced takeoff, hover, and landing systems riding wind currents

## 14. Autonomous Self-Driving Trucks
- heavy-duty truck control systems linking braking, throttle, and steering with TMS logic
- MVP: simulation environment or a basic assistive control module
- Complexity: L4
- Future: full-scale autonomy
