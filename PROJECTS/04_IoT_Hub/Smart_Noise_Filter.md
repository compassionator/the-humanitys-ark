# Smart Noise Filter

## Pitch
A wearable audio bridge. It sends ambient noise to the phone, filters out the annoying junk, and feeds only crucial sounds like horns or names to noise-canceling earbuds.

## Why
It solves daily sensory overload and focus issues without leaving the user deaf to the surroundings.

## MVP 1
Mic captures sound -> Bluetooth to phone -> simple classification -> output to earbuds.
