# Portable Tanboor Tuner

## Pitch
A custom, physical tuner built for real-world playing, not generic guitar-app logic.

## Why
It is personal, straightforward, and the perfect first proof that a real device can be built and shipped.

## MVP 1
Detect pitch and show tuning clearly in a portable form.
