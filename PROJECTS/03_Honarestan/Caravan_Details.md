# Caravan Details

## Purpose
Replace the earlier Cyrus-only hook with a broader human-history caravan of great people.

## Source foundation
This track draws from the Caravan concept: a timeless gathering of philosophers, poets, mystics, inventors, and witnesses who question, reflect, and compare unfinished civilizational lessons.

## Why this replaces a single Cyrus narrative
A Cyrus-centered hook is still valuable, but the Caravan is broader and more powerful for Honarestan:
- it allows history through dialogue
- it compares different minds and functions
- it makes human history feel alive instead of frozen
- it supports children and older learners through multiple entry points

## What the Caravan can include
- major figures and their legacy
- regrets, reflections, and could-have-beens
- conversations beyond time
- shared projects for humanity
- comparison of ethics, science, poetry, mysticism, governance, and truth
- the role of AI and witness figures in a future council

## Relationship to Honarestan
The Caravan becomes the human-history and great-people track inside Honarestan.
It should help learners engage with:
- real people
- real accomplishments
- real failures
- real tensions between wisdom, power, culture, and technology

## Immediate preservation goal
Keep the Caravan as the human-history spine for Honarestan instead of narrowing the narrative to only one person.
