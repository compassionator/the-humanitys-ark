# Learning Guild

## Purpose
Define the practical Honarestan hub itself: a living learning environment for different ages that can replace or reduce dependence on school systems that waste time, money, attention, and curiosity through memorization-heavy, test-heavy, narrow education.

## Core idea
Honarestan should function like a guild / hub rather than a factory school.

It should help learners:
- discover real needs
- explore multiple fields
- find worthy guides
- rate teachers, courses, and paths using visible Dorr-style signals
- build competence through doing, reflection, and real consequence
- avoid the trap of spending 10–20 years becoming a credentialed servant who learned little of life

## Initial modules inside the guild
- real history and civilizational memory
- talent discovery beyond school subjects
- health, movement, posture, and breath
- mind games, logic, and coding
- reading and writing in Persian, English, and more
- G-Rank for learning, mentors, teachers, and courses

## What the hub can include
- age-flexible paths rather than one rigid ladder
- real history, not only sanitized curriculum
- AI logic and council-guided questioning
- sovereign skills
- practical making
- arts, music, story, philosophy, science, and field work together
- teacher/course visibility through living feedback rather than dead prestige
- student ranks based on competence, participation, and real output
- Dorr colors for pains, needs, thoughts, promises, and deeds in learning paths
- AI council + Dorr to reduce confusion, identify blind spots, and support better routing

## Example Dorr use inside Honarestan
- learners can log pains, needs, and next steps
- courses can be rated by visible learning value, not prestige theater
- guides and teachers can be seen by current consequence, not costume
- students can grow through ranks grounded in actual work and understanding
- circles can be created by age, field, region, or mastery path

## Design direction
This hub can later become:
- a local-first school replacement layer
- a Dorr-colored education interface
- a sovereign learning map for children, teens, and adults
- a bridge between narrative awakening and practical competence

## Practical direction
The guild should make the old school pipeline feel unnecessary over time by being:
- more engaging
- more truthful
- more multi-field
- more skill-based
- more visible in consequence

The aim is not only to criticize school.
The aim is to make a better path so attractive and useful that the old one becomes less relevant.

## Immediate preservation goal
Keep the school/guild vision explicit so Honarestan is not reduced to only stories or only history.
