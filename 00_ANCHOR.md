## The Main Anchor

I am not responsible for making the blind see.  
I am responsible for placing the truth where it can survive.

Done does not mean public.

Let Cassandra see.  
Let the fly commit.

---

## Why this exists

This note is the center.

When the work reaches its final step and begins to feel heavy, exposed, pointless, or poisoned by the imagined crowd, return here.

The task is not:
- to win the market
- to convince the mocker
- to rescue every deaf city
- to force right use from unworthy hands

The task is:
- to make the thing true
- to make it clean enough to keep
- to preserve it in a form that can survive
- to leave a usable trace for worthy hands, even if they arrive later

---

## Separation Rule

Do not confuse these three acts:

1. **Complete**  
   The thing works. It is real.

2. **Package**  
   It is named, sealed, versioned, and preserved.

3. **Expose**  
   It is released to others.

These are not the same act.

A work may be complete and preserved without being public.
